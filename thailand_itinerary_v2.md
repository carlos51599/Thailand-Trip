| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |

#### Malaria

- **Low risk** in tourist areas (Bangkok, Chiang Mai, islands)
- **Higher risk** in border areas, rural jungle
- **Khao Sok:** Low risk but take precautions (repellent, long sleeves at dusk)
- **Consult GP** for your specific itinerary

#### Dengue Fever

- **Present in Thailand** - no vaccine widely available
- **Prevention:** Mosquito repellent (DEET/Picaridin), especially dawn/dusk
- **Symptoms:** High fever, severe headache, joint pain - seek medical attention
